The Global Education Market Landscape

The international education sector is a dynamic global industry. Understanding its trends in Bangladesh and worldwide is key to strategic success.

Bangladeshi Students Abroad: Rapid Growth

The number of Bangladeshi students pursuing education overseas has surged, from 49,150 in 2020 to over 70,000 in 2022, highlighting a strong and growing demand for international academic experiences.

Source: EduExpress International Report, based on market data.

Global Student Mobility: US Market Snapshot

The US remains a top destination, hosting over 1.1 million international students in 2023-2024, who contributed $43.8 billion to its economy. While India and China are major sources, countries like South Korea and Brazil show steady growth. Bangladesh ranks in the top 8 source countries for the US.

Conceptual representation of major source regions for US international students.

Motivations for Study Abroad

Bangladeshi students primarily seek overseas education for better career prospects, access to world-class learning environments, and personal development through cultural adaptation.

Future Market Trends & EduExpress's Scope

  • Shifting Preferences: Focus on affordability, ROI, and post-study work.
